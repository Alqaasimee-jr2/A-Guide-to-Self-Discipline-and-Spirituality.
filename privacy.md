# Privacy Sanctuary

At **A New Horizon**, we value the sanctity of your focus and the privacy of your journey.

## 1. Information Collection
We collect only what is necessary to deliver your digital items:
- **Email Address**: To send you the *8-Week Compass* and our weekly momentum reflections.
- **Analytics**: Basic, anonymous usage data to improve the sanctuary experience (via privacy-first analytics).

## 2. Your Data
- **No Sharing**: We do not sell, rent, or trade your data with third parties. Ever.
- **Ownership**: You remain the master of your information. You may unsubscribe or request data deletion at any moment.

## 3. Communication
- You will receive the *8-Week Compass* upon signup.
- You will receive weekly reflections designed to save you time and provide clarity.
- We will never clutter your inbox with noise.

## 4. Contact
For matters regarding your data privacy, you may reach out through the sanctuary channels.
